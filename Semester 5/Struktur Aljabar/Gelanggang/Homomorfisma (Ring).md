#Definisi 

Misalkan $R_1, R_2$ gelanggang. Fungsi $f:R_1 \to R_2$ disebut **homomorfisma** jika $\forall a, b \in R_1$:
1.  $f(a + b) = f(a) + f(b)$
2.  $f(a\cdot b) = f(a) \cdot f(b)$
